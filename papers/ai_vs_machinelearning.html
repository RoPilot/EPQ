Automation has been used for many years by many organisations and industries to transform how they work and run, however, AI and Machine Learning has been created as a new Technology, which offers a way to automate decisions, create a human like way of thinking, and complete tasks to a huge scale, in shorter, more cost effective ways. Netflix uses automation to allow users to login, upload new shows and movies, and manage payments. AI and Machine Learning has been used at Netflix to allow a greater user experience, where the user can now have recommendations based on what they enjoy to watch, alike shows and what a vast number of viewers watch at any 1 time. Without AI, no human like decisions, such as a viewers movie preferences or viewing habits would be processed in an efficient way. AI is all about making businesses and tasks scalable, in an efficient way.
